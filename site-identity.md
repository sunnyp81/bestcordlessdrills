## Audience (ICP)

UK homeowners and renters doing occasional-to-regular home DIY (flat-pack, shelves, curtain rails, decking, small renovations), plus a keen-DIYer segment assembling a proper tool kit and a smaller trade-curious segment. Situation: mid-purchase, weighing a budget own-brand tool against a premium trade brand, unsure whether the premium is worth it for their wall types (UK brick and masonry) and their actual usage, often already tied to one battery platform. The job they hire the site for: tell me, honestly and specifically for the UK, whether I should spend more, which battery platform to buy into, and which retailer own-brand line is genuinely fine versus a false economy. Sophistication: moderate, wants plain-English decoding plus one defensible pick and the reasoning behind it.
